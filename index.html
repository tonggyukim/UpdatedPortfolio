About Me

Hello! I am a recent Biology Graduate from the University of Washington. I initially started school with aspirations to go to med school and become a doctor. However, my interests had changed so I took a year off from school in 2018-2019 to try out different things like coding. I took University of Washington's coding bootcamp and thats when I discovered a new passion for programming. As a college student, I was the youngest among my classmates taking the course, and while everyone else was applying for jobs after graduating, my family and I thought it would be the best to finish my degree in Biology before I started job searching. While continuing college, I self taught myself and focused more on software engineering and testing projects. Without an official degree in computer science, I may seem not as qualified as those that have one. However, after hopefully viewing the projects I have created, a hiring employer can see that I am just as if not more knowledgeable in the field as my prospective interns/employees.
